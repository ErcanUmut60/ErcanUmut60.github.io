Welcome

I'm Ercan Umut Danışan, an Aerospace Engineer and M.Sc. candidate at Middle East Technical University (METU). Welcome to my personal portfolio website.

My academic and professional work is heavily centered around Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD). Specifically, my research explores unsteady adjoint-based shape optimization and the mitigation of Ignition Overpressure (IOP) and acoustic noise during rocket launches using the open-source solver SU2.
